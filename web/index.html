'ReadingBox' Free Library

About Us

Why Choose Us ?

'ReadingBox' is a bilingual (Sinhala and English) Free library for everyone who lives in Sri Lanka. With your dedicated request, it will come to your neighborhood and we will help you to build it.
If you are looking forward to improving your reading and literacy knowledge freely, this is the concept you should share with your neighbors. We also encourage you to participate in our knowledge building workshops as well as writing competitions.

Who we are

'ReadingBox' is a non-profit Free library service for everyone. The Ultimate objective of ReadingBox is to improve reading skills and practice good reading habits among society. To be a member, Please submit membership form. An after six months of your voluntary service you will be automatically assigned a team leader.

Shape Your Community with ReadingBox

Our Motivation

Our Vision

'Reading maketh a full man', so said the famous poet Francis Bacon. In this modern computer age, people are beginning to read less and less. With the idea of encouraging reading, specially among the young, we have come up with a new concept, 'The Reading Box'

Our Vision

Importance

Reading is important; because words, speak and written form are building blocks of life. People, families, every relationships and even nations are built from words. Reading develops the mind. The mind is a muscle. It needs exercise.

A person who does not read is like a 'Frog in a well'. He is unaware of what is going on in the rest of the world.

Importance

What helps

Reading helps to develop one's imagination and it helps to discover new things. In short, reading helps an individual to reach greater heights. It opens out the world to him. In this modern age where people are getting away from books, we thought of stepping in through the Reading Box, to encourage people to read books instead of wasting their time on frivolous pursuits that are available to them in the modern age.
